# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- constructive technical feedback
- respectful disagreement
- inclusive language
- professionalism in issues, PRs, and discussions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- hate speech or discriminatory language
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent participation restrictions.
